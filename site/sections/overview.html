Overview

Framing the problem, who we're solving it for, and its impact

Who Are We

Faraday Motors is an up-and-coming electric car startup that is revving up production of our first vehicle. We are data scientists trying to help all aspects of business be driven by information and wisdom gleaned from data.

What's the Problem

Without any prior sales data, our marketing team has no historic information to base marketing strategies on. Additionally, constrained by a limited budget, they wish to make efficient and effective data driven decisions to find optimal markets and leads.

What's the Impact

Our company's success is highly dependent on how many people buy our cars. Capitalizing quickly on the first mover advantage by effective marketing will help us survive, compete and grow against the bigger players planning to offer their own electric cars.
